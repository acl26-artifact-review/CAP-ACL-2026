Chief Complaint
Cough and upper respiratory infection

History of Present Illness

He denies fever, shortness of breath, or chest pain. He reports taking over-the-counter cough medicine, but it hasn't provided significant relief. The patient also mentions feeling generally unwell and fatigued. The doctor assesses an upper respiratory infection.

Findings
Examination reveals clear lung sounds bilaterally. The patient appears mildly fatigued. No fever detected.

Assessment
The patient presents with an upper respiratory infection and a cough.

Plan
The doctor recommends coricidin hbp for symptomatic relief. The doctor recommends cough medicine. Follow up in one week if symptoms persist.
