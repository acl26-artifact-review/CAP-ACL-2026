Chief Complaint
Left shoulder pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports left shoulder pain for the past month, worsening with overhead activities. He denies a specific injury. He reports using ibuprofen with minimal relief. He states the pain is constant and limits his ability to perform daily activities.

Findings
The patient's left shoulder range of motion is limited. Palpation reveals tenderness to the anterior aspect of the shoulder. Strength testing reveals a 3/5 score for abduction and external rotation.

Assessment
The patient's presentation is most consistent with subacromial impingement syndrome.

Plan
Prescribe ibuprofen 600mg every 6 hours as needed for pain. Refer to physical therapy for evaluation and treatment. Schedule follow-up in 2 weeks to assess response to treatment.
