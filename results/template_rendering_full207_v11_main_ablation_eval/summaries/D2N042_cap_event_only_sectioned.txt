Chief Complaint
Right ankle pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports right ankle pain, stating it was caused by twisting his ankle while playing basketball. He rates the pain as 6/10 currently. He denies any numbness or tingling. He reports using ice packs and elevating his foot.

Findings
Physical exam reveals moderate tenderness to palpation over the dorsomedial aspect of the right ankle. Range of motion is limited due to pain. No signs of neurovascular compromise. The right ankle demonstrates mild edema.

Assessment
Right ankle sprain, mild.

Plan
Prescribe an air cast for the right ankle. Recommend ice application to the right ankle. Order crutches for weight-bearing. Prescribe ibuprofen 600mg every 6 hours as needed for pain. Schedule a follow-up appointment in 14 days.
