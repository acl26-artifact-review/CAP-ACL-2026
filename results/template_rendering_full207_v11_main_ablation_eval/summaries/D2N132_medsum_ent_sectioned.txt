Chief Complaint
Back pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports back pain following a fall during a surprise birthday party, where he was crunched behind others while hiding. He describes twisting his back while jumping up and possibly twisting during the fall. He denies any pain shooting down his legs. He reports some stiffness with rotation and no evidence of radiculopathy. He has a prior lumbar fusion and takes ibuprofen for anxiety. He continues to take motrin 800mg three times a day with food.

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness to palpation of the lumbar spine. The patient reports pain with rotation. X-ray demonstrates a previous lumbar fusion at T4, with no evidence of acute compression fractures or significant abnormalities. There is no evidence of nerve compression.

Assessment
The patient has a strain of the lower back.

Plan
Continue the motrin at 800mg three times a day with food. Use ice and heat alternating on the back. Schedule a follow-up in one week if symptoms worsen or do not improve. Dragon, please order the medications and procedures and close out the note.
