Chief Complaint
Right elbow pain and tenderness.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports a history of anaphylaxis and has been taking ibuprofen for ongoing pain. He reports tenderness around his lateral epicondyle and pain with elbow flexion and extension. He also describes swelling and redness in the area. He underwent neck surgery previously. He currently carries an epinephrine auto-injector.

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness over the lateral epicondyle and pain on the dorsal aspect of the forearm. X-ray of the elbow demonstrates mild lateral elbow swelling but no fracture.

Assessment
Lateral epicondylitis.

Plan
Prescribe ibuprofen 800 mg every six hours as needed, provide a sling for comfort, and recommend motrin for pain relief. Follow up in four weeks if symptoms persist.
