Chief Complaint
Pain in the arm following hitting it on the net.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports pain in his distal radius following hitting his arm on the net, with pain exacerbated by thumb stress and flexion. He also reports some swelling and asks about numbness in his hand. He denies any numbness. He reports allergies and takes flonase. He has no prior surgeries.

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness over the distal radius of the arm. The x-ray of the arm shows no fractures or abnormalities.

Assessment
The patient has a distal radius strain and contusion.

Plan
Recommend ice and anti-inflammatory medication, and prescribe motrin 800 mg three times daily with food. Follow up in four weeks if symptoms persist.
