Chief Complaint
Concern about potential STD exposure and vaginal discharge.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports concern about potentially contracting an STD and states she is concerned about her boyfriend's infidelity. She reports a change in vaginal discharge over the last week and a change in vaginal odor currently. She also reports vaginal pain. The patient had an irregular pap smear approximately 20 years ago. She reports having multiple partners over the past three months.

Findings
Examination reveals vaginal pain.

Assessment
The patient's symptoms are consistent with a possible sexually transmitted infection.

Plan
The patient will be tested for Hepatitis C, gonorrhea, chlamydia, trichomoniasis, syphilis, HIV, and syphilis. The patient will also have an annual checkup. Follow up in four weeks if symptoms persist.
