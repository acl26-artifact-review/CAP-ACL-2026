Chief Complaint
Headaches

History of Present Illness
The patient reports headaches, primarily located around the forehead and temples, for the past month. He describes them as dull and persistent, with occasional sharp spikes. He rates the pain as 6/10 on a typical day. He denies fever, stiff neck, or visual disturbances. He reports that the headaches are exacerbated by stress and lack of sleep. He is currently taking New Balance shoes and Asics shoes. He recommends Nike shoes.

Findings
Neurological exam is unremarkable. Cranial nerves are intact. Range of motion is full. No tenderness to palpation. Blood pressure 130/80 mmHg, heart rate 72 bpm, temperature 98.6°F.

Assessment
Idiopathic intracranial hypertension is concerning. The patient reports headaches, blurred vision, and ringing in the ears.

Plan
Order MRI of the brain to evaluate for idiopathic intracranial hypertension. Start caffeine as needed for headache relief. Follow up in two weeks to assess response to treatment.
