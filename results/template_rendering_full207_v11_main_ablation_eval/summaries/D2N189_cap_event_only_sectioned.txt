Chief Complaint

Findings

Assessment
Chest pain, etiology uncertain. Differential includes musculoskeletal pain, angina, and anxiety.

Plan
Order cardiac enzyme panel, stress test, and schedule follow-up with cardiology in one week. Advise patient to avoid strenuous activity until further evaluation.
