Chief Complaint
Patient reports a cough and a concerning lung nodule discovered during a chest x-ray.

History of Present Illness
The patient initially presented with a persistent cough, which began approximately three months prior to the visit. The cough was initially mild but gradually worsened, particularly after loading bills for a horse stable. He reports a subsequent cold exacerbating the cough. The patient reports no current shortness of breath or chest pain. He quit smoking seven years ago and reports feeling better since. He has a history of rheumatoid arthritis, currently managed with methotrexate 7.5 mg weekly, and reports good control with minimal flares.

Findings
Physical exam revealed blood pressure of 120/76 mmHg, heart rate of 70 bpm, respiration rate of 16 breaths per minute, and oxygen saturation of 98% on room air. The patient is afebrile. Lung auscultation revealed clear and equal bilateral lung sounds. Examination revealed some swelling to the knuckles on the third digit of each hand, consistent with rheumatoid arthritis. Pulses are intact and capillary refill is brisk. A chest CT prior to the visit revealed a solitary 2 cm nodule in the lateral aspect of the right upper lobe, which appears smooth and without evidence of emphysema.

Assessment
The patient has a solitary 2 cm lung nodule in the right upper lobe, requiring further evaluation. He also has a history of rheumatoid arthritis.

Plan
I am ordering a pulmonary function test to assess lung function. I am also scheduling a PET-CT scan to determine the metabolic activity of the nodule. I recommend video-assisted thoracoscopic removal of the nodule, with general anesthesia and an estimated procedure time of 1.5 to 2 hours. I will admit the patient to the hospital for one night following the procedure and arrange for a chest tube to be removed at bedside. I will follow up with the patient in four weeks, or sooner if needed. I will also continue to coordinate care with the patient's rheumatologist and encourage adherence to his current methotrexate regimen.
