Chief Complaint
Left elbow pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports pain in the right elbow, primarily with flexion and extension, and reports tenderness to palpation. He states the pain began yesterday following lifting a heavy box and reports swelling. The patient also reports throbbing pain and redness in the right elbow. He has a history of anaphylaxis.

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness to palpation over the right elbow, with limited range of motion. No swelling is immediately apparent.

Assessment
The patient is diagnosed with lateral epicondylitis and an elbow injury.

Plan
The doctor orders a sling and orders motrin. Follow up in four weeks if symptoms persist.
