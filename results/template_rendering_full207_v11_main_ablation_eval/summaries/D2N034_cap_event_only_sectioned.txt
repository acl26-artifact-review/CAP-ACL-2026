Chief Complaint
Left shoulder pain.

History of Present Illness

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness of the greater tuberosity of the humerus. The patient has no tenderness at the sternoclavicular or acromioclavicular joints. The patient has limited active and passive range of motion.

Assessment
Clinical presentation is most consistent with rotator cuff tendinopathy.

Plan
