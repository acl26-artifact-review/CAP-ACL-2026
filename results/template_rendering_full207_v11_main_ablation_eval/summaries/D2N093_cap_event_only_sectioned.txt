The patient reports shortness of breath. The patient reports shortness of breath.

Findings
The patient's respiratory rate is 20 breaths per minute. Oxygen saturation is 94% on room air. Lung sounds are clear bilaterally with no wheezes or rales. The patient's blood pressure is 130/80 mmHg. The patient's glucose level is 200 mg/dL.

Assessment
The patient presents with acute shortness of breath, likely secondary to underlying type 2 diabetes and potentially exacerbated by exertion. Further evaluation is needed to rule out other causes.

Plan
Start metformin 1000mg twice daily for type 2 diabetes. Order a chest X-ray to evaluate for pulmonary causes of shortness of breath. Schedule follow-up in 24 hours to assess response to treatment and further investigate.
