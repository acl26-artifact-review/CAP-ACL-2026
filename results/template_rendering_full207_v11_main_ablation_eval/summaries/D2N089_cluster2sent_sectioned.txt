Chief Complaint
Patient reports fatigue and palpitations.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports fatigue for the past two months, accompanied by palpitations. She describes these palpitations as stress-exacerbated, particularly when engaging in activities that cause stress. The patient reports having tried meditation and running with her dog to relieve stress. She denies recent flare-ups of reflux or chest pain, shortness of breath, nausea, or vomiting. She also reports nasal congestion due to allergies.

Findings
The clinician observes a 2/6 systolic ejection murmur. The clinician observes a regular rate and rhythm. The clinician observes clear lungs. The clinician observes edema and erythema on the right elbow, with tenderness to palpation. The event monitor shows conversion pauses during episodes of atrial fibrillation.

Assessment
The patient presents with palpitations and fatigue, concerning for possible cardiac etiology.

Plan
The clinician plans a cervical MRI to evaluate for cervical radiculopathy. The clinician plans to refer the patient to cardiology for cardiac ablation. The clinician plans to continue the patient on methotrexate 2.5 mg once weekly. The clinician plans to continue the patient on protonix 40 mg a day.
