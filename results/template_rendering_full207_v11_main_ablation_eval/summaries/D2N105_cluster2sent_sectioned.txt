Chief Complaint
Patient reports fatigue and leg pain.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports fatigue for the past two months, accompanied by leg pain for approximately one month. He denies blood in stool and reports no chest pain or fever. He reports symptoms have improved 70-80% in the last six months. The patient reports feeling dizzy and takes a pill for dizziness.

Findings
The patient reports a new worrisome heart murmur and irregular heartbeats. The patient reports swelling in the ankles. The EKG reveals frequent PVCs and an anterior septum infarct pattern.

Assessment
The patient presents with symptoms suggestive of aortic stenosis and possible cardiac ischemia.

Plan
Order an echocardiogram to evaluate for aortic stenosis. Clinician plans to continue current medications. Clinician suggests deferring statin therapy. Clinician orders a cervical MRI to evaluate for cervical radiculopathy. Clinician suggests considering TAVR.
