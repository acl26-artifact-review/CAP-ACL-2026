Chief Complaint
Left knee pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports pain when getting from a seated position to standing and when going up and down stairs. He describes the pain as deep and achy. The patient reports hearing creaking in his knees. The patient reports knee pain and leg pain. The patient reports pain after watching TV. The patient previously took ice. The patient initially experienced pain. The patient takes aleve.

Findings
The doctor heard a regular heart rate and rhythm with no murmurs. The patient denies swelling or redness in their knees. The patient has normal gait.

Assessment
The patient has patellofemoral pain syndrome.

Plan
The patient will receive physical therapy. The doctor recommends treadmill running. The patient will take ibuprofen as needed.
