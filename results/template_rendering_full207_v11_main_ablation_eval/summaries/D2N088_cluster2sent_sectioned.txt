Chief Complaint
Fatigue and shortness of breath.

History of Present Illness

The patient denies any history of seasonal allergies and reports feeling short of breath primarily when lifting or carrying things. The patient reports coughing up nothing currently, but anticipates coughing. The patient reports feeling a little warm but attributes it to exertion. The patient reports elbow pain bilaterally and knee pain.

Findings
The patient's hemoglobin A1c is elevated at 8%.

Assessment
The clinician suspects a viral syndrome.

Plan
The clinician orders a COVID test. The clinician recommends robitussin and ibuprofen. The clinician suggests psychotherapy as an option for depression. The clinician increases metformin to 1000 mg twice daily. The clinician orders a hemoglobin A1c in four months. The clinician orders a lipid panel. The clinician orders a refill of lisinopril.
