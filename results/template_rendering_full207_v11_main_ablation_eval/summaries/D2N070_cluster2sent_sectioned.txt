Chief Complaint
Back pain following lifting heavy objects.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports back pain following lifting heavy objects. He also reports tingling in his toes on the right foot and prefers lying on his right side for sleep. The patient admits to inconsistent weekend adherence to his diet and reports blood pressure readings are slightly elevated at home on weekends. He has a past history of knee osteoarthritis.

Findings
The clinician hears a systolic heart murmur. The clinician finds pain to palpation of the lumbar spine and notes pain with flexion and extension of the back. The clinician notes a normal x-ray of the back.

Assessment
The patient presents with acute low back pain, likely musculoskeletal in origin, and possible peripheral neuropathy.

Plan
Prescribe meloxicam 15 mg once daily for back pain. Increase metformin to 1000 mg twice a day for diabetes. Repeat a hemoglobin a1c in 6 months for diabetes. Increase lisinopril from 10 mg to 20 mg daily for hypertension.
