Chief Complaint
Left wrist pain.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports pain in the left wrist, describing it as sharp and intermittent. He states the pain began approximately one week ago after a fall while gardening. He rates the pain as 7 on a scale of 10. He also reports pain radiating down into the forearm, and pain in the left elbow. The patient reports pain that extends down into the forearm. The patient reports pain in the left hand. He has been doing a lot of pottery and ceramics in the past week.

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness to palpation over the distal radius and ulna. Range of motion is limited due to pain. No swelling is noted.

Assessment
The patient is experiencing medial epicondylitis and pain in the left wrist.

Plan
Order wrist x-rays. Prescribe ibuprofen 600mg every 6 hours as needed for pain. Instruct the patient to apply ice to the wrist three times daily. Schedule a follow-up appointment in one week to assess progress.
