S:
The patient reports persistent right elbow pain, exacerbated by gripping and lifting. He states the pain started approximately two weeks ago after yard work. He denies any recent trauma. The patient reports using over-the-counter ibuprofen with minimal relief.

O:
Exam reveals tenderness to palpation over the medial epicondylitis area. Range of motion is limited by pain. Grip strength is decreased on the right hand. No swelling or erythema is noted.

A:
The patient's symptoms and exam findings are consistent with medial epicondylitis.

P:
The doctor instructs the patient to apply ice to the elbow three times daily. The doctor recommends physical therapy for strengthening exercises.
