Chief Complaint
Chest pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports chest pain, describing it as a dull ache located in the center of his chest. He states the pain started approximately three months ago and has been intermittent, worsening with exertion. He denies any recent trauma. He reports associated shortness of breath and nausea. The patient also reports walking and bowel changes. He has a history of high blood pressure and diabetes. He is currently taking metformin.

Findings
Blood pressure is 142/88. Hemoglobin A1c is 7.0%.

Assessment
The patient presents with chest pain and a history of high blood pressure and diabetes. He is likely experiencing cardiac symptoms.

Plan
Start blood pressure medication. Order monitoring devices. Recommend diet control. Follow up in four weeks.
