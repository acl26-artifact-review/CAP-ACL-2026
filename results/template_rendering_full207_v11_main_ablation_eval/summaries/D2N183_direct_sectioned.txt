Chief Complaint
Follow-up for acne.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports that he is here for a follow-up regarding his acne. He reports that his acne is improving on the face, but he has experienced some flare-ups on his chest and back. He has been using clindamycin lotion, tretinoin cream, and benzoyl peroxide wash as directed. He reports no significant irritation from the medications. He has not consistently used the benzoyl peroxide wash on his chest and back.

Findings
Examination reveals scattered inflammatory papules and pustules across the upper back and posterior shoulders. There are fewer than five inflammatory papules on the forehead, cheeks, and chin. The acne on the face appears improved and well-controlled. The back demonstrates a mild flare.

Assessment
Acne vulgaris, with improvement on the face and a mild flare on the back.

Plan
Continue current treatment plan for the face. Initiate treatment with PanOxyl 10% foaming wash to the back in the shower daily. Order a one-year prescription for clindamycin lotion with 11 refills and a prescription for tretinoin cream with three refills. Send prescriptions to Walgreens on Broad Street. Provide samples and coupons for PanOxyl 10%.
