Chief Complaint
Scalp irritation and itching.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports experiencing scalp irritation and itching for approximately six months. He also reports experiencing dandruff. He has previously tried Head and Shoulders and castor oil to manage these symptoms. The patient denies any recent trauma or other causes for the scalp irritation.

Findings
Examination reveals mild erythema and scaling of the scalp.

Assessment
The patient's symptoms and examination findings are consistent with a diagnosis of seborrheic dermatitis.

Plan
Continue Head and Shoulders shampoo daily. Follow up in four weeks to assess response to treatment.
