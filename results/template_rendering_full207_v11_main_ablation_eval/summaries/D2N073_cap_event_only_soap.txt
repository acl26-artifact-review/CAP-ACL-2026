S:
The patient reports pain in the left hand, describing it as a dull ache that is constant. He states the pain started approximately one week ago after a minor fall. The patient denies any tenderness to palpation. He reports no numbness or tingling. The patient reports no fever or chills.

O:
Blood pressure is 120/80 mmHg. Heart rate is 72 bpm and regular. The left hand is warm and pulses are present. Range of motion is full and without pain. No swelling or erythema is noted.

A:
The patient's symptoms are most consistent with a musculoskeletal strain of the left hand.

P:
Rescribe ibuprofen 600mg every 6 hours as needed for pain. Instruct patient to apply ice pack to the affected area for 20 minutes at a time, several times a day. Advise patient to avoid activities that aggravate the pain. Schedule follow-up in 2 weeks if symptoms do not improve.
