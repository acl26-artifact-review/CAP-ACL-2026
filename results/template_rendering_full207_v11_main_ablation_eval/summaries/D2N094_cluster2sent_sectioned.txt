Chief Complaint
Right index finger pain.

History of Present Illness

Findings
The right hand x-ray revealed no fractures and a sprain of the distal interphalangeal joint of the right index finger.

Assessment
The patient has a sprain of the right index finger.

Plan
Order a strong antiinflammatory medication called mobic and a splint for the right finger. Schedule a follow-up appointment in two weeks to assess healing.
