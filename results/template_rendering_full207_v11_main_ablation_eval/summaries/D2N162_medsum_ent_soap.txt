A:
The patient's symptoms are consistent with a right ankle sprain. There is no fracture on x-ray.

P:
I recommend keeping the leg elevated, continuing icing, and using crutches for the next one to two days. Prescribe NSAIDs as needed for pain. Schedule a follow-up appointment in two weeks to assess progress and determine readiness for return to soccer activity. Advise patient to call the clinic if symptoms worsen or do not improve.
