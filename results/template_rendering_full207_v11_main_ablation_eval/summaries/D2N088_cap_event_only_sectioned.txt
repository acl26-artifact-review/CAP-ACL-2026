Chief Complaint
Left elbow pain and windedness when lifting.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports a history of type two diabetes and hypertension. He also reports a history of depression. He states he's been feeling tired and short of breath, particularly when lifting or carrying heavy bags. He denies any history of seasonal allergies. He reports elbow pain and knee fatigue. He also reports nausea. He denies vomiting. He is currently taking medication for depression and monitors his sugar levels while working.

Findings
Examination reveals windedness when lifting and carrying heavy bags. The patient reports elbow pain and knee fatigue. The patient reports tension around the knees.

Assessment
The patient's symptoms and examination findings are consistent with musculoskeletal issues, including possible rotator cuff tendinopathy and osteoarthritis.

Plan
Order shoulder radiographs to evaluate for rotator cuff pathology. Initiate physical therapy for musculoskeletal issues. Continue acetaminophen as needed for pain relief. Schedule follow-up in four weeks to assess response to treatment.
