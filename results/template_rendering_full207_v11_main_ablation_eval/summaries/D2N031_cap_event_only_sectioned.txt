Chief Complaint
Right breast skin thickening

History of Present Illness
The patient reports skin thickening at the medial inferior aspect of the right breast. He denies any recent trauma or changes in the skin. He reports a history of a right breast lumpectomy last year and subsequent radiation therapy. His previous mammogram in January 2021 was negative. He has been observed to have no recurrence since his lumpectomy and radiation therapy.

Findings
Physical exam reveals skin thickening at the medial inferior aspect of the right breast. No other findings on physical exam.

Assessment
The patient has stage zero DCIS and no evidence of recurrence.

Plan
The patient should continue to be observed. Order follow-up imaging in six months.
