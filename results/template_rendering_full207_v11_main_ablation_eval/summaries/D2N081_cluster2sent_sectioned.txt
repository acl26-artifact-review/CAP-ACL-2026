Chief Complaint
Fatigue and sore throat.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports fatigue for the past two months, with no specific inciting event. He also reports a sore throat, which he describes as lingering. He denies blood in stool, nausea, vomiting, or shortness of breath. He reports not eating as much as he normally would. He reports tolerating nausea with current chemotherapy.

Findings
The patient's oxygen saturation is observed to be good. Examination reveals no cervical lymphadenopathy or supraclavicular adenopathy. The heart rate and rhythm are regular. Crackles are heard bilaterally in the lungs. Erythema is observed on the anterior chest. Mild radiation pneumonitis is noted.

Assessment
The patient presents with fatigue and a sore throat, likely related to chemotherapy and radiation therapy.

Plan
Continue current chemotherapy and radiation dose. Prescribe low-dose prednisone for symptom management. Prescribe lidocaine swish and swallow. Schedule repeat imaging to monitor for radiation pneumonitis. Follow up in four weeks.
