O:

A:
The patient presents with persistent abdominal pain and urinary symptoms, suggestive of a possible inflammatory or infectious process. Further investigation is warranted to rule out structural abnormalities and assess the underlying etiology of her symptoms. Given her family history of kidney disease, a thorough evaluation of her renal function is crucial.

P:
Order a urinalysis to evaluate for protein and blood. Recommend continued adherence to Gatorade and water intake. Schedule a follow-up appointment in one month to monitor symptoms and assess renal function. Consider referral to a pediatric nephrologist for further evaluation and management.
