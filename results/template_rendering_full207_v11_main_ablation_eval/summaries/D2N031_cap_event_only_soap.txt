The patient's skin thickening in the right breast is concerning for possible malignancy, given his history of lumpectomy and radiation therapy. Differential includes DCIS, invasive carcinoma, and benign etiology.

Order a full-breast mammogram and ultrasound to further evaluate the skin thickening. Schedule follow-up in 2 weeks to discuss results and plan for further management.
