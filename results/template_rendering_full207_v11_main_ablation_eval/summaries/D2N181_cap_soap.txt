The primary problem is gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD). The patient's symptoms and history are consistent with GERD. The patient's stress and anxiety may be contributing factors. A family history of esophageal cancer is present.

P:
Rescribe a prescription for nexium 40 milligrams twice daily to be sent to the patient's pharmacy. Recommend an upper endoscopy (egd) to evaluate for potential causes of GERD. Schedule a follow-up appointment in 4 weeks to discuss the results of the endoscopy and assess response to treatment. Advise the patient to avoid trigger foods, such as spicy foods, citrus products, and carbonated beverages. Consider a trial of protonix if omeprazole is not sufficient.
