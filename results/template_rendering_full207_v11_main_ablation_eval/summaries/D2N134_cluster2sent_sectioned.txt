Chief Complaint
Fatigue and joint pain.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports fatigue for the past two months, accompanied by joint pain in her elbows. She also reports nausea, loss of appetite, and body aches. The patient describes a bull's-eye rash and fever. She reports elevated blood sugar levels. She is currently taking metformin and lisinopril.

Findings
Examination reveals a bull's-eye rash. Blood glucose is elevated.

Assessment
The patient's symptoms and physical exam findings are consistent with Lyme disease.

Plan
Prescribe doxycycline, order a hemoglobin A1c, and order a lipid panel. Schedule a cervical MRI to evaluate for cervical radiculopathy.
