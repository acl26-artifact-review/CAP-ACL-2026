Chief Complaint
Left leg pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports pain in the top part of his right leg, which began yesterday after a fall while bowling. He also reports bruising on the back end of the left hand and pain in the left hand. He denies any swelling. The patient reports difficulty walking and avoids stairs. He reports pain with walking.

Findings
Physical examination reveals tenderness to palpation in the right calf. No swelling is noted.

Assessment
The patient's presentation is most consistent with a contusion and pain with walking.

Plan
The doctor recommends the patient use ice. The doctor will start the patient on mobic 15 milligrams once a day.
