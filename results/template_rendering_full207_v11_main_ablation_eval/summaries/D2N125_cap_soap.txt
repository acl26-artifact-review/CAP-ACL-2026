Cotton tape testing reveals decreased sensation on the right side of the face. An MRI reveals a small blood vessel abutting and perhaps pinching the trigeminal nerve.

A:
The patient's symptoms and physical exam findings are consistent with trigeminal neuralgia. The doctor suggests the patient may have trigeminal neuralgia.
