Chief Complaint
Right middle finger pain and tenderness.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports pain and tenderness in his right middle finger following a rear-end collision four to five days ago. He describes the pain as sharp and constant, exacerbated by movement. He reports experiencing some anger during the accident. The patient also reports a hand injury and a distal phalanx fracture of the middle finger. He reports very much so pain.

Findings
Examination reveals tenderness to palpation over the distal phalanx of the right middle finger. The patient has a hand injury.

Assessment
The patient has a distal phalanx fracture of the middle finger and a hand injury.

Plan
The patient will receive a finger splint. The patient will receive tramadol 50 mg every 6 hours as needed for pain for two days. The patient will have a follow-up x-ray in two weeks.
