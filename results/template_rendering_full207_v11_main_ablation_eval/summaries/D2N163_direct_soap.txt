The patient has been diagnosed with hypercholesterolemia. The elevated cholesterol level warrants further investigation to determine the underlying cause.

P:

Schedule a follow-up appointment in 3-6 months after meeting with a registered dietitian to discuss dietary changes. Recommend continued physical activity, including cycling. Follow up with a repeat cholesterol level after 3-6 months.
