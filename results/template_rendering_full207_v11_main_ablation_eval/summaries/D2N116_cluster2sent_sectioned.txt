Chief Complaint
Left shoulder pain.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports left shoulder pain for several weeks following backyard work. He also reports a history of right shoulder pain related to baseball playing. He denies numbness in the arm or hands. The patient reports taking Advil and icing the shoulder.

Findings
The clinician assesses the patient's right shoulder as having limited range of motion and tenderness at the greater tuberosity. The patient reports foot soreness following yard work and trying ice and elevation for the feet.

Assessment
The patient is diagnosed with rotator cuff tendinopathy.

Plan
The clinician orders an MRI of the right shoulder. The clinician recommends physical therapy for six to eight weeks and suggests considering a steroid injection if symptoms persist.
