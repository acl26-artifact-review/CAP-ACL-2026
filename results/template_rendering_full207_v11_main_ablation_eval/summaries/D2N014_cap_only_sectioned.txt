Chief Complaint

History of Present Illness

He reports playing tennis in hot and humid conditions in the past. The patient reports no vision problems.

Findings
The doctor observed high blood pressure at 160/95. The echocardiogram shows left ventricular hypertrophy. The echocardiogram pumping function is stable. The ECG is stable. The patient reports dizziness.

Assessment
The patient's symptoms and examination findings are consistent with heart failure exacerbation, alongside musculoskeletal pain in the left hand. He has a history of a possible heart attack and skin cancer.

Plan
Start heart failure medications as ordered. Order shoulder radiographs. Initiate physical therapy for the left hand pain. Follow up in one week to assess response to treatment. Advise patient to avoid strenuous activity until further evaluation.
