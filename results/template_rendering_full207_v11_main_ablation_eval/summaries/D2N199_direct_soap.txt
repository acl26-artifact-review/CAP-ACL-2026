A:
The patient's symptoms are consistent with a viral syndrome with possible bronchitis and a right medial collateral ligament strain from the fall this morning. His hypertension is currently well-controlled. His diabetes is not being actively managed.

P:
Rescribe guaifenesin 600 mg twice daily for cough. Prescribe meloxicam 15 mg once daily for right knee pain. Order right knee x-ray. Order lipid panel. Schedule physical therapy referral for right knee. Instruct patient to monitor blood sugars and report to the patient portal. Continue lisinopril 20 mg daily. Schedule follow-up in 2 weeks to review x-ray and physical therapy progress.
