Chief Complaint
Shortness of breath

History of Present Illness
The patient reports experiencing shortness of breath, particularly during mild exercise and walking up her driveway. She feels it may be related to back pain. She has experienced this more lately. She reports experiencing moments of low mood, and has been taking effexor for depression. She admits to a couple of cigarettes a day, and has been trying to quit. She reports back pain and uses gabapentin for it, which helps. She wishes she was doing yoga for her back pain, but hasn't been consistent. She reports mild wheezes on exam.

Findings
Physical exam reveals mild wheezes in all lung fields. The patient's diaphragm appears flattened. Examination also notes mild back pain.

Assessment
The doctor diagnoses the patient with COPD. The patient may have COPD.

Plan
The doctor will order combivent (two puffs twice a day). The doctor will order albuterol (as needed). The doctor will order a prednisone taper pack. The doctor prescribes prednisone.
