He also reports pain with wrist extension against resistance.

O:
Physical exam reveals moderate tenderness to palpation of the medial epicondyle. Range of motion is limited by pain. Vitals are within normal limits. X-ray shows no fracture or bony abnormality.

A:
The patient's symptoms are consistent with medial epicondylitis.
