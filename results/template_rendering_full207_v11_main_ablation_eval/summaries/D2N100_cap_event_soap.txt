The chest and back are relatively spared. The remainder of the physical exam is normal.

A:
The patient has mild to moderate acne with a predominantly comedonal presentation and a small inflammatory component.

P:
Start with a noncomedogenic moisturizer and sunscreen SPF 30 or higher daily. Apply adapalene 0.1% cream to affected areas a few nights a week, gradually increasing to nightly. Use the elf facial cleanser and persa-gel as directed. Follow up in six months to assess progress.
