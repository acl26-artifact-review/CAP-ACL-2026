Chief Complaint
Left shoulder pain

History of Present Illness
The patient reports left shoulder pain for six weeks, exacerbated by overhead activities and lying on the affected side. He denies recent trauma and reports partial relief with acetaminophen. He also reports fatigue and shortness of breath with exertion.

Findings
The patient reports windedness when lifting or carrying heavy bags. Examination reveals tenderness to palpation over the left shoulder. The patient reports tension around the knees.

Assessment
The patient's presentation is most consistent with musculoskeletal pain, likely related to underlying fatigue and shortness of breath. He has a history of depression and type two diabetes.

Plan
Prescribe robitussin for symptomatic relief. Prescribe ibuprofen and acetaminophen for pain management. Prescribe metformin 1000 milligrams twice daily for type two diabetes. Order a hemoglobin A1c in four months for type two diabetes. Schedule follow-up in one week to assess response to medications and physical therapy referral.
