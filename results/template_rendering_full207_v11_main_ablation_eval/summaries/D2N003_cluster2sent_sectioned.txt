Chief Complaint

He reports some muscle aches, likely related to favoring his back due to pain. The nurse performed a review of systems, noting blood in the urine, body aches, chest pain, and general muscle aches. He has had no recent trauma.

Findings
The patient's blood pressure is elevated. Physical exam reveals cDA tenderness on the right-hand side. Creatinine was drawn due to concern for kidney stones. Abdominal x-rays show a possible kidney stone. The nurse noted tenderness to palpation of the right lower quadrant.

Assessment
The patient is a 61-year-old male with a past medical history of kidney stones, migraines, and reflux, presenting with back pain, likely a recurrence of his kidney stones.

Plan
