Chief Complaint
Fatigue for the past two months.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports fatigue for the past two months, accompanied by feelings of stress and anxiety related to the holidays. He also reports difficulty maintaining a diet over the holidays. The patient denies blood in stool, chest pain, or shortness of breath, and reports no weight loss.

Findings
The clinician hears a slight two out of six systolic ejection murmur. The clinician observes one plus non-pitting edema bilaterally in the lower extremities. The EKG shows no concerning findings. The patient's hemoglobin A1c is elevated.

Assessment
The patient presents with fatigue and elevated hemoglobin A1c, suggesting possible underlying metabolic or endocrine issues.

Plan
Increase metformin dosage, order a lipid panel, and order an echocardiogram. Follow up in four weeks to assess response to treatment and further investigate the cause of the fatigue.
