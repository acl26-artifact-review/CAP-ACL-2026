Chief Complaint
The patient reports ongoing pain in his right knee for approximately two months.

History of Present Illness
The patient reports swelling in his right leg and knee for approximately two months, accompanied by pain when straightening his knee fully. He reports that compression socks have not been helping with the swelling and also reports soreness in the area of an old bruise.

Findings
The clinician observes inflammation in the knee.

Assessment
The patient presents with a right knee, exhibiting pain and swelling.

Plan
The clinician plans to administer a steroid injection to the knee, obtain an x-ray of the knee, refer the patient to physical therapy, and obtain a consultation for surgery if needed. The clinician recommends reducing salt intake, elevating the feet, and following up with the patient's private care physician. The clinician plans to monitor the patient's CMP levels every two months.
